WHO ARE WE?

Peak Streetwear is a brand of urban clothes created by Eric Cartman in 1985, when he realized that skaters had to constantly buy new clothes, especially shoes. So he designed the first five products thinking about durability and style. Since then, the only objective of Peak Streetwear was to offer the best option for skaters, bikers and all urban athletes, in order to have the possibility to buy less than two or three pair of shoes per month.

Eric Cartman

Nowadays, we are proud to say that we have more than two thounsand employees, 20 all around the United States, and stores all over the world. Thank YOU, for choosing Peak Streetwear, none of this would have benn possible without you.
